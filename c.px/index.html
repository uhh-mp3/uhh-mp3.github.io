What is Standoff 2?

Standoff 2 continues the tradition of the first part in the new first-person shooter format for the series. Choose the mode, grab your favorite gun, and soon enter the battle!

Battalions take place all over the world: from narrow streets somewhere in Italy to a secret laboratory in the mountains. In addition to the new maps for the series, you will find the return of the already classic Training Outside and Arena in their updated form.

There's something to fight. More than 20 weapons models are fully available for selection from the start of the game. Take what you like and cut the frags!

Favorite gun can be decorated as you want: the game a huge variety of skins and stickers. Experiment and show off your combinations in front of other players!

You can also boast about your skills. Take part in competitive matches, where not only victory is at stake, but also your own rating. Can you win the Legend rank?

With his team to play not only more convenient, but also more fun. Create your own clan and invite friends to play with the whole company!

And all this in a variety of modes:

The bomb laying.
Team fight.
Arms race.
Sniper duel.
Go to Standoff 2, pump your skills, earn rank and get seasonal rewards!
